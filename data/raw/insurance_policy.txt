SECTION 1: WAITING PERIODS AND IMMEDIATE COVERAGE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1.1 STANDARD WAITING PERIOD
All policyholders must complete a mandatory 6-month waiting period 
before submitting claims for pre-existing medical conditions. Pre-existing 
conditions include any illness or injury diagnosed or treated within 
48 months prior to policy purchase.

1.2 IMMEDIATE COVERAGE
The following are covered immediately from policy start date with zero 
waiting period:
- Accidental injuries and emergency procedures
- Ambulance services
- ICU admissions due to accidents
- Emergency room visits

1.3 SPECIFIC WAITING PERIODS
- Maternity coverage: 12 months waiting period
- Cataract surgery: 24 months waiting period
- Joint replacement (non-accident): 24 months waiting period


SECTION 2: SURGICAL COVERAGE AND BENEFITS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2.1 ORTHOPEDIC PROCEDURES
Knee surgery, hip replacement, and spinal procedures are covered at 
80% of actual hospital bills. Coverage eligibility:
- Accident-related cases: Covered after 3 months of active policy
- Elective surgeries: Require minimum 2 months policy duration
- Emergency orthopedic surgery: Immediate coverage

2.2 CARDIAC PROCEDURES
Angioplasty, bypass surgery, and valve replacement covered at 90% of 
actual costs. Minimum policy duration: 6 months for elective procedures.
Emergency cardiac procedures covered immediately.

2.3 TYPICAL APPROVED AMOUNTS
Based on network hospital rates:
- Knee arthroscopy: ₹80,000 - ₹1,20,000
- Knee replacement: ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,50,000
- Hip replacement: ₹2,00,000 - ₹3,00,000
- Cardiac angioplasty: ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,00,000


SECTION 3: GEOGRAPHIC COVERAGE AND NETWORK HOSPITALS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3.1 TIER 1 CITIES (100% Coverage)
Treatment in network hospitals in the following cities covered at 100% 
of scheduled amounts:
- Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai

3.2 TIER 2 CITIES (80% Coverage)
Treatment in Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Kochi, Chandigarh covered at 
80% of scheduled amounts.

3.3 OTHER LOCATIONS (70% Coverage)
All other cities and towns covered at 70% of scheduled amounts when 
treated at empaneled hospitals.

3.4 NON-NETWORK HOSPITALS
Treatment at non-network hospitals covered at 60% of scheduled amounts 
with prior approval required.


SECTION 4: AGE-BASED ELIGIBILITY AND REQUIREMENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4.1 STANDARD ELIGIBILITY (Ages 18-50)
Policyholders aged 18-50 years are eligible for all surgical procedures 
without additional screening. Standard premium rates apply.

4.2 SENIOR ELIGIBILITY (Ages 51-65)
Policyholders aged 51-65 require:
- Additional medical evaluation before policy issuance
- 20% higher premium
- Some exclusions may apply based on health screening

4.3 SENIOR CITIZEN (Ages 65+)
Limited coverage available:
- Emergency procedures only
- No elective surgeries
- 50% higher premium
- Maximum coverage capped at ₹3,00,000 per year


SECTION 5: CLAIM LIMITS AND FINANCIAL CAPS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5.1 ANNUAL LIMITS
- Maximum total claims per policy year: ₹5,00,000
- Maximum per hospitalization: ₹2,00,000
- Maximum outpatient expenses: ₹50,000 per year

5.2 SUB-LIMITS
- Room rent limit: ₹5,000 per day (single AC room)
- ICU charges: ₹10,000 per day
- Ambulance charges: ₹2,000 per trip

5.3 CO-PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS
No co-payment for ages 18-50 in network hospitals.
10% co-payment for ages 51+.


SECTION 6: EXCLUSIONS AND NON-COVERED ITEMS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- Cosmetic or aesthetic procedures
- Experimental or unproven treatments
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Injuries due to participation in hazardous sports
- Treatment for drug or alcohol addiction
- Dental procedures (unless accident-related)
- Vision correction surgeries


SECTION 7: CLAIM SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

7.1 DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED
- Original hospital bills and discharge summary
- Doctor's prescription and treatment notes
- Diagnostic test reports
- Policy document copy
- Government-issued ID proof
- Bank account details for reimbursement

7.2 SUBMISSION TIMELINE
Claims must be submitted within 30 days of discharge.
Late submission may result in 10% reduction in approved amount.
